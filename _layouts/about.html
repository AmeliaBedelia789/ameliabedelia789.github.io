About Us

Meet the founders of Backyard Barns: Amelia and Violet.

Our Story

Amelia and Violet, friends and hobby horse enthusiasts, started Backyard Barns to share their love for the sport. They organize events, competitions, and summer camps for hobby horse riders of all ages. You will often see Violet riding on one of her 12 hobby horses, and Amelia on one of her two hobby horses. They enjoy jumping and trail riding as much as barrel racing and playing around.
